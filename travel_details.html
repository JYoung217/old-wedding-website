Flying to Chicago

We recommend for most guests to fly into Chicago's O'Hare International Airport (ORD). ORD is located in a northwest suburb and while further away from Chicago's downtown, it is only a 25-minute drive to the reception and ceremony. It is Chicago's largest airport and services most domestic and international airlines.

Another option for domestic travelers is Midway Airport (MDW). MDW is located in Chicago's southwest side and serves exclusively Southwest and Delta airlines. While only a 25-minute drive from downtown Chicago, it will take approximately 45 minutes to drive to the ceremony and reception in ideal traffic conditions.
