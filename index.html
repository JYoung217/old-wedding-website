Our Story

October 18, 2009 - Jon and Lexi first crossed paths in the hallway of Northwestern's Plex dormitory during their sophomore year of college. No words were exchanged, but Jon remembers thinking, "Wow, she's cute!" Lexi does not remember the encounter.


November 4, 2009 - Jon and Lexi were first introduced to one another at a Fall Overnight Retreat held by Northwestern's Asian American InterVarsity Christian Fellowship. They were tasked with cooking dinner during the first night of the retreat. Lexi remembers Jon as being tall, a little nerdy looking, and very quiet. Jon does not remember the encounter.


February 17, 2010 - Lexi brought Jon a mini-cake to celebrate his birthday. Jon was touched and immediately believed that Lexi liked him. In reality, Lexi was just being nice.


March 2010 - When Lexi was sick in her dorm room, Jon brought over a homemade concoction of peanut butter and hot chocolate. After just one sip, Lexi fell in love. Jon does not remember this occurrence.


May 2010 - Jexi's first date (which Lexi did not know was a date until after the fact). They ate authentic pho. When Jon went to the restroom, the bill came and Lexi paid. Jon was mortified.


June 21, 2011 - After spending a year apart to pray and reflect, Jon surprised Lexi at the airport with a collection of handwritten signs (think "Love Actually" sign scene) that ended in him asking her to be his girlfriend. She said yes.


July 18, 2015 - After 4 wonderful years of dating, Jon prepared a picnic on the beautiful shores of Northwestern's lakefill and brought Lexi along for a day of kite-flying. As they walked around the water's edge, Jon rallied his courage, got down on one knee, and asked Lexi to be his wife. Once again, she said yes.


We are two imperfect people, brought together by God's grace alone, and greatly blessed through one another. We hope and pray that our union will bring honor and glory to the God who has saved us and given us so much. And we are excited to see where He leads us next.
