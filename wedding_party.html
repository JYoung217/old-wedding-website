The Beautiful Ladies

Shelley Xu - Maid of Honor

The Unicorn Princess Twin - Shelley met Lexi one fateful night only two years ago. Shelley brought wine to a bible study group Lexi was in, together they played a rousing game of Quelf, and the rest is history. After two wonderful years of being in the same bible study group, Shelley has consistently been an encouragement to Lexi, both in her spiritual walk and her fashion sense. Now roommates, Shelley and Lexi enjoy lounging together in their matching unicorn onesies, and rejoicing in the sisterhood that God has blessed them with.

Kathryn Choi - Bridesmaid

The Conjoined Queen - After meeting at Northwestern and bonding over classy wine dates on Friday nights (Kathryn usually being the classy one), Kathryn and Lexi have since been inseparable. Literally. Whether living in the same house or just within the same apartment complex, Kathryn has shared a roof with Lexi for the past four years. Needless to say, this has made for many a girls' night of yummy food, movies, prayers, and stories of how God is moving and working in their lives (and, of course, more wine).

Alex Wright - Bridesmaid

The Classic Frenemy - The first time Alex saw Lexi in 10th grade Geometry class, she heard Lexi laugh and hated her immediately (Alex specifically wanted to emphasize this point of hatred). Luckily, through God's gracious humor, Alex and Lexi went on to bond over Speech and Debate and eventually became BFFs. When Lexi came to Christ in college, Alex was one of the first people to rejoice with Lexi, and despite being miles and miles apart, they have continued to speak God's love over one another ever since.

Helen Chang - Bridesmaid

The Hugging Convert - In high school, Helen was always Lexi's favorite person to hug. Not because Helen enjoyed it, but precisely because she found it absolutely deplorable. Nevertheless, after years of heartfelt conversations (not to mention some ridiculously goofy ones as well), and being miles apart during college, Helen has finally relented and now allows Lexi to hug her freely. She even drops an occasional "love you" in conversations!

Wai Yan Lam - Bridesmaid

The Original Boochi - Wai Yan and Lexi have not seen each other since high school (which is really quite crazy to think about). And yet, somehow, their high school bonds over Speech and Debate, stressful AP classes, Student Government, boy drama, and (of course) Hello Kitty have stood the test of time. Their affection for one another cannot be summed up by any known words, and so they have had to coin their own pet name: they lovingly refer to one another simply as "Boochi".

The Strapping Gentlemen

Christopher Eleiott - Best Man

Chris is Jon's closest friend in Chicago. Despite only meeting a little over a year ago, they hit it off right away when Jon found out that Chris's ideal drink to have with a fancy dinner was the same as his: chocolate milk. A true character out of "Up In The Air", Chris's job takes him all over the country and despite all this, he remains Jon's most trusted confidant.

Hyesung Oh - Groomsman

Hyesung "Hace" Oh is Jon's oldest and best friend from Northwestern University. Having gone through all the peaks and valleys of growing in the Christian faith and the college experience together, each time they get together feels like they can pick up from where they left off last despite being separated by many miles. A modern-day Richard Aoki, Hace motivates Jon everyday to stand firm in what he believes in.

Kun Chen - Groomsman

Despite starting off with a checkered history, Jon and Kun's friendship has really grown over the past eight years, showing that nothing can overcome the work of Christ in each of their lives. Each time they are together on the frisbee field, however, they must show each other up. Though Kun is miles away as a medical student in New York City, he continues to be one of Jon's closest friends.

John Lee - Groomsman

Jon's life has been immeasurably heightened having been friends with John for many years. John personifies what it means to be a genuine friend by constantly checking in on Jon and ensuring his well-being. In spite of being separated by thousands of miles and having not seen each other in years, John was the very first person Jon asked to be a groomsman for the wedding.

Charlie Santilukka - Groomsman

Jon first met Charlie at a small group Bible study several years ago and did not care for him upon their first meeting. This quickly changed as Jon spent more time with him - he and Lexi even attended Charlie's wedding! Charlie has proven time and time again to be a divinely inspired voice of reason in Jon's life and continues to be one of Jon's most trusted spiritual mentors and teachers.
